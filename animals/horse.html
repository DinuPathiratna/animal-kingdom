horse

The horse is a domesticated equine mammal valued for its strength, speed, and adaptability. Horses have a strong, muscular body, long legs, and are herbivores that eat grass, leaves, and sometimes fruit. They are social animals often living in herds or groups.

Scientific Classification

Kingdom Animalia
Phylum Chordata
Class Mammalia
Order Perissodactyla
Family Equidae
Genus Equus
