cheetah

The cheetah is the fastest land mammal in the world. It has a long, slender body covered in yellowish fur with distinctive small black spots. Its build — including long thin legs, a flexible spine, and long tail — is highly adapted for speed and agility. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
