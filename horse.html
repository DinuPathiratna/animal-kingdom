horse

The horse is a domesticated equine mammal valued for its strength, speed, and adaptability. Horses have a strong, muscular body, long legs, and are herbivores that eat grass, leaves, and sometimes fruit. They are social animals often living in herds or groups. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
